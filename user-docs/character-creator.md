# Character Creator Guide

Learn how to create unique AI agent personalities with our interactive Character Creator.

## What is a Character?

A character defines your AI agent's personality, knowledge, and behavior. Think of it as giving your AI a unique identity - how it talks, what it knows, and how it responds to users.

## Creating Your First Character

### Using the AI Assistant

The easiest way to create a character is with our AI assistant:

1. Go to [Character Creator](/dashboard/character-creator)
2. Click "Create New Character"
3. Start a conversation with the AI assistant
4. Answer questions about your character:
   - **Name**: What should we call your agent?
   - **Purpose**: What will it help users with?
   - **Personality**: Formal or casual? Friendly or professional?
   - **Knowledge**: What topics should it know about?

The AI will generate a complete character definition for you in real-time!

### Example Prompts

Try these example prompts to get started:

- "Create a helpful customer support agent for an online store"
- "Make a creative writing assistant that helps with story ideas"
- "Build a fitness coach that motivates and gives workout advice"
- "Design a technical documentation writer"

## Character Properties

### Basic Information

- **Name**: Your character's name (e.g., "Alex the Assistant")
- **Username**: Handle for your character (e.g., "@alex")
- **Bio**: A brief description of who they are

### Personality Traits

Define your character's personality with:

- **Adjectives**: Keywords like "friendly", "professional", "witty"
- **Topics**: Areas of expertise like "technology", "health", "finance"
- **Style**: How they communicate (formal, casual, emoji-filled)

### Conversation Examples

Provide examples of how your character should respond:

- User: "Hello!"
- Character: "Hi there! How can I help you today?"

These examples help the AI understand your character's voice.

## Editing Characters

You can modify any character you've created:

1. Go to [My Agents](/dashboard/my-agents)
2. Click on a character to edit
3. Use the AI assistant or manual editor to make changes
4. Save your updates

Changes take effect immediately!

## Advanced Features

### Import/Export

- **Export**: Download your character as a JSON file
- **Import**: Upload a character file to reuse or share

### Plugins

Add special capabilities to your character:

- **Web Search**: Let your agent search the internet
- **Image Generation**: Enable image creation
- **Knowledge Base**: Upload documents for your agent to reference

### System Prompts

For advanced users, customize the core instructions that guide your AI's behavior.

## Best Practices

### 1. Be Specific

Instead of: "Make a helpful bot"
Try: "Create a customer service agent for a coffee shop that's friendly and knows about different coffee types"

### 2. Provide Examples

Show the AI how your character should talk by giving conversation examples.

### 3. Test and Iterate

After creating a character:
1. Test it in the chat interface
2. Note what works and what doesn't
3. Refine the personality
4. Test again

### 4. Keep It Focused

Characters work best when they have a clear purpose. Don't try to make one character do everything!

## Sharing Characters

Want to share your creation?

1. Go to character settings
2. Enable "Public"
3. Your character will appear in the [Marketplace](/marketplace)
4. Other users can try it or use it as a template

## Tips for Great Characters

- **Define clear boundaries**: What topics should they handle? What should they avoid?
- **Add personality**: Make them memorable with unique traits
- **Use examples**: Show don't tell - provide conversation samples
- **Test thoroughly**: Chat with your character before deploying
- **Iterate**: Characters can always be improved based on user feedback

## Need Inspiration?

Visit the [Marketplace](/marketplace) to see characters created by other users. You can test them and learn from their design!

## Troubleshooting

### My character isn't responding how I want

Try being more specific in your character definition. Add more conversation examples showing the desired behavior.

### The AI assistant isn't understanding me

Break down your request into smaller parts. Instead of describing everything at once, answer the AI's questions one by one.

### I want to start over

You can always create a new character or reset your current one by clicking "New Character".
