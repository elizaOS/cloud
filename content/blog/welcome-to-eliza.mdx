# Welcome to Eliza Cloud

We're excited to announce the launch of Eliza Cloud, a platform designed to make deploying and managing AI agents easier than ever.

![AI Technology Dashboard](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1551288049-bebda4e38f71?w=1200&h=600&fit=crop)

## What is Eliza Cloud?

Eliza Cloud provides a seamless experience for:

- **Deploying AI agents** with just a few clicks
- **Managing your agents** through an intuitive dashboard
- **Scaling automatically** based on demand
- **Monitoring performance** in real-time

![Cloud Infrastructure](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1544197150-b99a580bb7a8?w=1200&h=600&fit=crop)

## Getting Started

Getting started with Eliza Cloud is simple:

1. Sign up for an account
2. Create your first agent
3. Configure your agent's personality and capabilities
4. Deploy and start chatting
