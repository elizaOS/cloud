# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about elizaOS Platform.

## Getting Started

### What is elizaOS Platform?

elizaOS Platform is a complete solution for building and deploying AI agents. You can create custom AI personalities, generate content, and integrate AI into your applications.

### Do I need coding experience?

No! Our visual Character Creator and dashboard make it easy for anyone to create AI agents. Developers can use our API for advanced integrations.

### How do I get started?

1. [Sign up](/login) for a free account
2. Get 100 free credits to explore
3. Try the [AI Chat](/dashboard/eliza)
4. Create your first character with the [Character Creator](/dashboard/character-creator)

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! New users get 100 free credits to test all features. No credit card required to start.

## Credits & Billing

### How much do credits cost?

Credits start at $10 for 1,000 credits. Larger packs offer better value. See [Pricing](/docs/pricing) for details.

### What can I do with 1,000 credits?

- 200-500 AI chat messages
- 100 image generations
- 20 short videos
- Plenty of API requests

### Do credits expire?

No! Your credits never expire. Use them whenever you're ready.

### Can I get a refund?

Yes, for unused credits within 30 days of purchase. Contact support to request a refund.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets through Stripe.

## Characters & Agents

### What is a character?

A character defines your AI agent's personality, knowledge, and behavior. It's like giving your AI a unique identity.

### How many characters can I create?

You can create unlimited characters on all plans!

### Can I share my characters?

Yes! Make your character public in the settings, and it will appear in the [Marketplace](/marketplace).

### Can I use someone else's character?

Yes, if they've made it public. You can test it or use it as a template for your own.

### How do I make my character better?

- Be specific about personality and purpose
- Provide conversation examples
- Test and iterate based on responses
- Check out the [Character Creator Guide](/docs/character-creator)

## API & Integration

### How do I get an API key?

Go to [API Keys](/dashboard/api-keys), click "Create New Key", and copy your key.

### Is the API free?

The API uses the same credit system as the dashboard. Check [Pricing](/docs/pricing) for rates.

### What's the rate limit?

60 requests per minute for standard users. Need more? Contact us about enterprise plans.

### Can I use the API in production?

Absolutely! Our API is production-ready and used by companies worldwide.

### Do you have SDKs?

Official SDKs are coming soon! For now, you can use standard HTTP requests. See our [API Guide](/docs/api-guide).

## Features

### What AI models do you support?

We support multiple models including:
- OpenAI (GPT-4, GPT-3.5)
- Anthropic (Claude)
- Google (Gemini)

You can select your preferred model in the chat interface.

### Can I generate images?

Yes! Use the [Image Generator](/dashboard/image) or the API. Images cost 10 credits each.

### Can I generate videos?

Yes! Our [Video Generator](/dashboard/video) can create short videos from text prompts.

### Can I upload my own data?

Yes! Use the Knowledge feature to upload documents that your AI agent can reference.

### Do you support voice/audio?

Voice cloning and text-to-speech features are available! Check out [Voices](/dashboard/voices).

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data safe?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your data is stored securely and never shared.

### Who can see my conversations?

Only you can see your private conversations. Unless you make a character public, it's completely private.

### Can I delete my data?

Yes! You can delete characters, conversations, and your entire account at any time from [Account Settings](/dashboard/account).

### Do you use my data to train models?

No! We do not use your data to train AI models. Your conversations remain private.

## Technical

### What happens if I run out of credits?

Your requests will pause until you add more credits. Your data and characters remain safe!

### Why is my response slow?

- High demand periods may cause delays
- Complex prompts take longer to process
- Video generation takes 1-5 minutes

### Can I cancel a generation in progress?

Yes, but the credits may still be deducted. Complex generations (video) cannot be cancelled mid-process.

### What browsers do you support?

We support all modern browsers:
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

### Is there a mobile app?

Not yet, but our web app works great on mobile browsers!

## Organizations & Teams

### Can I add team members?

Yes! Invite team members to your organization in [Organization Settings](/dashboard/organization).

### Do team members share credits?

Yes, all organization members share the same credit pool.

### Can I set permissions for team members?

Currently, all members have the same access. Granular permissions are coming soon!

### How many team members can I have?

No limit! Add as many team members as you need.

## Marketplace

### What is the Marketplace?

A place to discover and share AI characters created by the community.

### How do I publish to the Marketplace?

In your character settings, enable "Public". Your character will appear in the Marketplace.

### Can I sell my characters?

Not yet, but this feature is coming soon!

### Can I report inappropriate content?

Yes! Use the report button on any character or contact support.
