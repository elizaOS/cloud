# Pricing & Credits

Understand how pricing works on elizaOS Platform and manage your credits effectively.

## How Credits Work

elizaOS Platform uses a credit-based system. You purchase credits upfront and they're deducted as you use AI features.

### Why Credits?

- **Predictable Costs**: Know exactly what you're spending
- **Pay As You Go**: Only pay for what you use
- **No Subscriptions**: No monthly fees or commitments
- **Transparent**: See costs before each action

## Credit Costs

### Text Chat
- **Simple messages**: 1-2 credits per message
- **Complex conversations**: 3-5 credits per message
- **Long responses**: Up to 10 credits

### Image Generation
- **Standard image** (1024x1024): 10 credits
- **High quality**: 15-20 credits

### Video Generation
- **Short video** (< 5 sec): 50 credits
- **Medium video** (5-15 sec): 100 credits
- **Long video** (15-30 sec): 200 credits

### API Usage
Same rates as dashboard features. Check [API Reference](/docs/api-reference) for details.

## Cost Optimization Tips

### 1. Use Efficient Prompts
Shorter, clearer prompts use fewer credits.

### 2. Batch Operations
Generate multiple images at once instead of one at a time.

### 3. Choose the Right Model
Some AI models cost less than others. Check the model selector in chat.

### 4. Cache Common Responses
For API users, cache frequent responses to avoid regenerating.

### 5. Buy in Bulk
Larger credit packs offer better value per credit.

## Understanding Your Bill

Each transaction shows:

- **Feature Used**: Chat, Image, Video, etc.
- **Credits Used**: Exact amount deducted
- **Remaining Balance**: Your current credit balance
- **Timestamp**: When the action occurred

View complete history in [Analytics](/dashboard/analytics).

## Questions?

### How do I know how many credits an action will cost?

The platform shows estimated costs before you confirm actions in the dashboard. For API users, check the [API Reference](/docs/api-reference) for exact rates.

### Can I share credits with my team?

Yes! Organization owners can add team members who share the credit pool.

### What happens if I run out of credits?

Your requests will be paused. Simply purchase more credits to continue.

### Do you offer monthly subscriptions?

Not currently. Our credit system is more flexible and cost-effective for most users.

### Can I get a discount?

Volume discounts are available for enterprise customers. Contact sales for details.
