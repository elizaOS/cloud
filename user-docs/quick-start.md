# Quick Start Guide

Get started with elizaOS Platform in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through creating your first AI agent.

## Step 1: Create an Account

1. Visit [elizaOS Platform](/login)
2. Sign up with your email, wallet, or social account
3. Verify your email if required

## Step 2: Add Credits

To use AI features, you'll need to add credits to your account:

1. Go to [Billing](/dashboard/billing)
2. Choose a credit pack
3. Complete your purchase with a credit card

**Pricing**: Credits start at $10 for 1,000 credits. Most text conversations cost 1-5 credits.

## Step 3: Try the AI Chat

Start by testing the AI chat feature:

1. Go to [AI Chat](/dashboard/eliza)
2. Type a message and press send
3. The AI will respond to your message

This is a great way to see how AI agents work before building your own.

## Step 4: Create Your First Character

Now let's create a custom AI character:

1. Navigate to [Character Creator](/dashboard/character-creator)
2. Click "Create New Character"
3. Use the AI assistant to define your character:
   - What's the character's name?
   - What's their personality like?
   - What will they be used for?
4. The AI will help you build a complete character definition
5. Save your character when done

## Step 5: Deploy Your Agent

Once you're happy with your character:

1. Go to [My Agents](/dashboard/my-agents)
2. Find your character and click "Deploy"
3. Your agent will be live in minutes!

## Next Steps

Now that you have a working agent, you can:

- **Customize Further** - Edit your character's personality and responses
- **Generate Images** - Try the [Image Generator](/dashboard/image)
- **Create Videos** - Explore [Video Generation](/dashboard/video)
- **Use the API** - Get an [API Key](/dashboard/api-keys) to integrate your agent
- **Browse Marketplace** - Discover agents others have built
